What can Old Glory do for you?

Old Glory has the expertise you need for your next project. We make the grade when it comes to smart design and quality service. Our meticulous attention to detail ensures your project will exceed your expectations.

Our fleet of well maintained, state of the art equipment ensures your project is completed efficiently and accurately.

Grading & Clearing

Whether existing or new construction Old Glory can fulfill any of your grading needs. We can determine the proper elevations to fit your need. We do top soil, fill dirt and stone. We can backfill for foundations as well as pool installations or removal.

If you are plagued with overgrowth or unwanted vegetation including trees and stumps, We have the capability of restoring it back to better days.

Site Preparation

Old Glory is prepared to help your with your design and executing your plan. We can assist you with installation of a stone base for your shed, auguring for deck footings, grade and compact for a paver installation, trenching for utilities drainage etc.

Let us help you with your project.

Drainage, Turf & Irrigation

Surface water runoff can be problematic. Old Glory is capable of addressing these issues. We are also capable of the final touches including Grass seed or sod installation.

Old Glory is networked with a certified irrigation specialist who can fulfill any of your irrigation needs.

Fencing & Boundry Maintainance

In your more rural environments with either open spaces and wooded areas boundary management is difficult due to limited accessibility. Old Glory can assist you with this need.

Whether it is preparing for surveying, fencing or landscaping we can assist you with getting you access and grade preparation for your project. We will be glad to sub contract with others contractors in assisting with these projects.

Forestry Management

With the increased severity of storms in this area of the East Coast many property owners have experienced vast tree uprooting and damage. Many properties are just unsightly littered with stumps, downed trees and branches.

Old Glory can address all these issues with minimal impact to the forestry environment Old Glory has handled soil conservation and forestry grants as to the removal of invasive vegetation.

Stone Driveway Installation & Repair

Stone driveways are an economic solution to your driveway, parking and drainage needs. However they do take a beating over time and severe weather conditions. Multiple snow plowing events will take a toll with stone loss and puddle development. Old Glory will renew that mess by replacing stone, grading and compacting.
